## 1. PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

### What It Is (Elevator Pitch)

**StockFox is India's investment confidence platform** - starting with an AI research analyst that explains stocks in plain English, expanding into the full investment journey (advisory, execution).

We're building the "complete doctor" for retail investors - comprehensive diagnosis, clear recommendations, and transparent explanations at retail pricing (₹99/year per stock vs ₹50K/month traditional advisors).

### What It Does (Core Functionality)

**StockFox makes institutional-grade stock analysis accessible to retail investors by:**

1. **Analyzing comprehensively** - 200+ metrics across 11 critical segments (currently 4-5 segments with DFY interpretation, others available for self-analysis)
2. **Diagnosing clearly** - Personalized stock health scores based on your investment goals
3. **Explaining transparently** - Every insight backed by evidence (94% citation target), no black-box recommendations
4. **Teaching contextually** - Learn investing concepts while analyzing stocks, build skills over time

**Think:** The doctor for your portfolio - not just test results (Screener), not black-box prescriptions (Liquide), but complete diagnosis + reasoning.

### How It Works (3-Layer Architecture)

#### **Layer 1: Comprehensive Data Analysis**
- **200+ metrics** across 11 segments: Profitability, Financial Ratios, Growth, Valuation, Price & Volume, Technical Indicators, Broker Ratings, Ownership, F&O, Income Statement, Balance Sheet/Cash Flow
- **Real-time data** (5-minute delay) from verified BSE/NSE sources
- **Historical trends** (5-10 year analysis for context)

#### **Layer 2: Intelligent Interpretation**
- **AI-powered insights** using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology trained on 17 years of institutional research methodology (Vishal's Julius Baer expertise)
- **Segment scores** aggregated from individual metrics with clear logic
- **Currently:** 4-5 segments with full DFY (Done-for-You) interpretation, remaining segments provide data for self-analysis

#### **Layer 3: Personalized Recommendations**
- **Coming Soon:** Personalized Buy/Hold/Sell verdicts based on:
  - Your investment thesis (Value, Growth, Dividend, Quality)
  - Your risk tolerance (Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive)
  - Your time horizon (Short <1Y, Medium 1-3Y, Long 3Y+)
  - Your portfolio context (existing holdings, concentration)
- **Currently:** Users interpret segment scores to make decisions
