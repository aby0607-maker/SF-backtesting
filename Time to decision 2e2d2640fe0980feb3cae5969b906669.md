# Time to decision

```jsx
NON-LINEAR - Depends on entry point, conviction, and user archetype

TIME TO DECISION (3 Scenarios):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SCENARIO A: VALIDATION USE CASE (Fastest - Same Day)
User Entry Point: Already researching a specific stock, needs validation
Journey: Phase 1 (10 min analysis) → Phase 3 (simulator 5 min OR direct execution)
Time to Decision: 15 minutes to 2 hours (same session OR same day)
Example: "I'm considering TCS, let me validate" → Sees score 8.7/10, rank #2 → BUYS

Archetype: Speed-focused, experienced investors, validation seekers
Frequency: 30-40% of new users

SCENARIO B: EXPLORATION USE CASE (Medium - 5-7 Days)
User Entry Point: Open to ideas, exploring multiple options
Journey: Phase 1 (stock 1) → Phase 2 (analyze 3-5 stocks over days) → Phase 3 (validate top pick)
Time to Decision: 5-7 days (spread across multiple sessions)
Example: Week 1 analyze 5 stocks → Week 2 pick top 2 → Simulate → BUY

Archetype: Thoughtful investors, learners, building conviction
Frequency: 40-50% of new users

SCENARIO C: PORTFOLIO REVIEW USE CASE (Variable - Instant to Weeks)
User Entry Point: Reviewing existing holdings
Journey: Phase 1 (analyze holding) → Decision depends on output
Time to Decision: 
  • HOLD: Instant (thesis confirmed, no action)
  • SELL: 1-3 days (if red flags found, need exit plan)
  • ADD: 1-2 weeks (if thesis strengthening, plan tranche buying)
Example: "Check my TCS holding" → Score 8.7/10 → HOLD (2 min decision)

Archetype: Portfolio managers, systematic reviewers
Frequency: 20-30% of new users (will increase with portfolio sync in Phase 2)

KEY VARIABLES AFFECTING TIME:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. ENTRY CONVICTION:
   • High conviction (validating existing idea): Same day
   • Low conviction (exploring options): 1-2 weeks
   • No idea (learning mode): 3-4 weeks

2. CAPITAL AMOUNT:
   • Small (₹10-25K): Faster decisions (1-3 days)
   • Medium (₹50K-1L): More validation needed (5-7 days)
   • Large (₹2L+): Advisor consultation, simulator (1-2 weeks)

3. EXPERIENCE LEVEL:
   • Experienced (5+ years): Faster (same day to 3 days)
   • Intermediate (2-5 years): Medium (5-7 days)
   • Beginner (<2 years): Slower (1-2 weeks)

4. STOCK COMPLEXITY:
   • Blue-chip (TCS, HDFC): Faster (straightforward thesis)
   • Mid-cap growth: Medium (need more research)
   • Turnaround/Unprofitable: Slower (complex, may need advisor)

AVERAGE TIME TO FIRST DECISION (Blended):
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
- Median: 7 days (across all archetypes)
- Range: 2 hours (speed-focused) to 3 weeks (cautious learners)
- Mode: 3-5 days (most common pattern)

CRITICAL INSIGHT FOR INVESTORS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
We DON'T force users through a linear timeline.

Users self-select speed based on:
  ✓ Their entry point (validation vs exploration vs review)
  ✓ Their conviction level (high → fast, low → slow)
  ✓ Their capital at risk (small → fast, large → slow)
  ✓ Their experience (expert → fast, beginner → slow)

This flexibility is a FEATURE, not a bug:
  • Speed-focused users convert same-day (₹99 × 1-2 stocks)
  • Thoughtful users convert over weeks (₹199/month subscription)
  • Both paths are profitable, both create loyal users

Unlike competitors forcing one path:
  • Screener: Always slow (45 min to 1 week+ DIY per stock)
  • Liquide: Always fast (5 min black-box, but blind trust)
  • StockFox: User chooses speed based on their needs
```
