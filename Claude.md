# StockFox Documentation Repository

## Project Overview

StockFox is India's investment confidence platform - an AI-powered stock research and analysis tool that makes institutional-grade stock analysis accessible to retail investors. The platform provides comprehensive analysis across 200+ metrics, transparent evidence-backed insights, and contextual learning to help retail investors make confident, independent investment decisions.

### Core Value Proposition
- **Comprehensive Analysis**: 200+ metrics across 11 segments (Profitability, Financial Ratios, Growth, Valuation, Price & Volume, Technical Indicators, Broker Ratings, Ownership, F&O, Income Statement, Balance Sheet/Cash Flow)
- **Transparency**: 94% citation target - every claim backed by evidence
- **Accessibility**: Institutional quality at retail pricing (₹99/year per stock vs ₹50K/month traditional advisors)
- **Education**: Contextual learning integrated into the analysis workflow

### Positioning
"The complete doctor for your portfolio" - not just data (like Screener), not black-box recommendations (like Liquide), but comprehensive diagnosis + transparent reasoning.

## Target Audience

### Primary ICP
- **Age**: 22-35 years (sweet spot: 22-30)
- **Income**: ₹5L - ₹15L+ annually
- **Location**: Pan-India (Tier 1 and Tier 2/3 cities)
- **Profile**: Salaried professionals, entrepreneurs with disposable income for investing

### User Segments
- **Efficiency Seekers (30%)**: Want good decisions fast
- **Control Freaks (30%)**: Don't trust tips, want to verify everything
- **Validation Seekers (30%)**: Research on their own but need expert confirmation
- **Aspiring Learners (10%)**: Want to learn investing properly

### Anti-Personas (Who we don't serve)
- Day traders (intraday focus)
- HNIs with existing advisors (₹50L+ portfolios)
- Pure passive investors (only MF SIPs)
- Hardcoded tip-followers (no interest in understanding)

## Product Phases

1. **Phase 1 (Current)**: AI Research Analyst - comprehensive stock analysis
2. **Phase 2 (2027)**: Advisory & Personalization - personalized verdicts, human advisory marketplace
3. **Phase 3 (2028-2030)**: Full-Stack Platform - transaction execution, MF analysis, vernacular support
